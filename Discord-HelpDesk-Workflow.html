Reply & Resolve
#support — most common action
Staff replies directly in Mava dashboard. The reply appears inside the customer's private Discord thread in real-time. Mark resolved → thread auto-archives.
  • Open ticket in Mava triage queue
  • Type reply → Send (customer sees it instantly in Discord)
  • Assign to teammate if needed
  • Add internal tags for tracking
  • Mark resolved → thread archives + Mava analytics updated
🟠
Push to HubSpot
#support — CRM-worthy contacts only
When a ticket comes from a significant account or potential deal, staff manually pushes to HubSpot. Nothing syncs automatically — every push is deliberate.
  • In Mava ticket view → click "Send to HubSpot"
  • Creates/updates contact: name + email
  • Creates ticket: category, priority, source URL
  • Links back to Mava ticket for full history
🔵
Promote to Jira
#bug-reports & #feature-requests — Phase 3
Once Jira credentials are configured, action buttons will appear on each Discord card. Staff reviews and clicks to promote — nothing auto-creates.
  • Bug card: [🐛 Create Jira Bug] button
  • Feature card: [✓ Accept → Story] or [✗ Reject]
  • Bot calls Jira REST API → issue created instantly
  • Discord card updates with Jira key (e.g. GO-247)
Crisis Escalation

Human handoff — fast

A custom Node.js webhook bot fires the escalation — not Mava. It watches for the moment a customer's email is collected and fires immediately. On-call staff get a rich embed in #staff-escalations with everything they need.

🔥
Urgent ticket submitted in #support
Customer submits a ticket with Critical or High severity. Mava fires a ticket.creation webhook → custom bot logs the ticket ID as a pending escalation.
📧
Email collected → escalation fires immediately
Mava fires ticket.updated with firstCustomerMessageCreatedAt — the bot detects the email has been captured and fires the Discord webhook instantly. No delays, no polling.
📢
Embed posted to #staff-escalations
Bot POSTs to the Discord channel webhook. The embed (shown right →) includes the @on-call role ping, customer identity, severity, their issue description, and a direct Mava link.
👤
@on-call acknowledges + enters Mava thread
On-call staff member acknowledges in #staff-escalations, opens Mava, clicks the ticket link, and joins the private thread. The customer sees a human has responded.
⚡
Phase 3 — Slack also notified
Add Slack webhook in Mava Settings → Escalation. Same trigger fires both: Discord @on-call embed + Slack team channel notification. No code required.
