09 — Recommendations
Actionable Insights
Data-driven recommendations for product, marketing, and go-to-market strategy based on chat analysis.
1
Lead Marketing With PIP + Disconnect Protection
These two features generated the most excitement and are perceived as unique differentiators. PIP was called "clean," "huge," and a "game changer." Disconnect protection was immediately understood as solving a real pain point.
"Damn the transitions on PiP are so clean" · "so basically no need for rented SRT relays anymore"
2
Publish Battery & Heat Benchmarks Pre-Launch
Battery life was the #2 most-asked question (12+ instances). Streamers need confidence that 4-8 hour sessions are viable. Publishing real-world battery data across iPhone models will remove a major adoption blocker.
"battery usage and overheating testing results would be nice to hear about"
3
Offer a Free Tier or Trial Period
Strong demand for try-before-you-buy. Pricing was the #1 concern. A free tier with basic features (as mentioned: "2 destinations") plus a paid tier for PIP, DR, and multi-stream would follow the Moblin/IRL Pro freemium playbook while capturing more users.
"A free trial with full app functionality would be nice for people to try streaming" · "there is a free tier w/ 2 destinations"
4
Fix Mirror Bug + Add In-App Toggle
8+ viewers independently flagged the mirrored front camera. This is a simple but visible issue. Adding an in-app mirror toggle (rather than relying on iOS settings) would eliminate friction and show polish.
6+ unique users flagged "mirrored" within first 15 minutes of Jake's stream
5
Prioritize OBS/External Camera Integration
Power users repeatedly asked about connecting DJI cameras, USB-C external cameras, and using GO as a source in OBS. This bridges the gap between phone-only and backpack streamers — a huge potential market segment.
"Can you hook up a DJI Action 4 with this app?" · "Is there a way to connect it to OBS as a browser source?"
6
Pursue 3 Identified Inbound Leads
Three viewers expressed direct beta/partnership interest: (1) naivety — produces for a streamer going to a big international event, wants beta access for stress testing. (2) rellim — "GIMME ACCESS, I'd be down to test in SoCal, I do a lot of IRL." (3) sahilgoeshard — "I work with a content creator with 1M followers." Real-world beta testing = powerful testimonials.
3 organic inbound leads from a single stream — high conversion signal
7
Emphasize "Not a Backpack Replacement" Messaging
Several viewers confused GO with the $20K+ backpack or worried it would cannibalize. Clear positioning: GO is the phone-first entry point; the backpack is for broadcast-grade production. Different tiers, same ecosystem.
"damn so u gotta pay 30k for the backpack and the app too?" · "ngl overpriced as hell these tvu backpacks"
8
MrKeem as Primary Ambassador
Keem's demo generated 64% of all TVU-related messages and had 89% positive sentiment. His audience is technically engaged and asked sophisticated questions. A viewer literally said: "Keem explains things so well I'm gonna tell everyone about this app."
427 unique chatters · 502 TVU mentions · "I'm gonna tell everyone about this app"
